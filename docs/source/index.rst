Welcome to Trail On's documentation!
===================================

Trail On is a game made by CoolDevGA
that enables you to survive at the toughest of ocations.
To Download and more, Check `CoolDevGA Trail On's <https://gamedevga.wixsite.com/trailon>`_ website.
Trail On's GUI is simple and for the game to perform, one doesn't need a gaming computer.

Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including
how to do your :ref:`installation` of Trail On.

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
